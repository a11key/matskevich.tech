Battery Tracking System “Smart Battery”

A hardware–software solution for real-time battery monitoring and visualization. The system consists of two modules:
Data Collector — measures pack voltage, current, and temperature, and transmits the data via radio.
Receiver — connects to a PC via USB and uploads the received data.

The project also includes dedicated software for real-time visualization: it displays current measurements and plots dynamic graphs with adjustable scales.

Ion Wind Thruster

Designed and validated an ion wind thruster. Developed a theoretical model of the ion wind effect and predicted thrust dependencies.
Simulated the electric field using ElCad.
Derived an empirical formula describing the relationship between thrust, electrode spacing, and circuit current.

Spiral Phase Plate (DLW Lithography)

Modeled and fabricated phase plate via two-photon lithography. Analyzed with SEM and profilometer.
